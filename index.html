Our Story

Crafting Beauty
Since 1997

For over two decades, Roop Milan Salon has been Nikol's most trusted destination for premium hair and beauty care. What began as a neighbourhood salon has grown into a landmark of style and confidence, now serving you at 2 convenient locations across Nikol.

Our team of experienced stylists combines global techniques with a deep understanding of Indian hair textures and beauty preferences. We use only premium, internationally certified products to ensure every service delivers salon-worthy results that last. Cleanliness, comfort, and customer delight are not just values — they are our practice, every single day.
